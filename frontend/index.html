Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the JNHS Portal.

How do I log in to the portal?

Students use their LRN as both username and password. Parents use their email as username and their child's LRN as password. Contact the registrar if you don't have an account yet.

How are grades computed in the portal?

The portal follows the DepEd K-12 grading system. Written Works (60%) and Performance Tasks (40%) are combined per quarter, then transmuted using the formula: 50 + (Raw Score / 2).

Can I download my Report Card (SF9)?

Yes! Students and parents can download SF9 (Report Card) and SF10 (Permanent Record) as PDF files from the Reports section. Contact the registrar for assistance.

How do I enroll for the new school year?

Enrollment can be processed through the portal or by visiting the school. The registrar will assist with section assignment and enrollment confirmation.

Who do I contact for portal issues?

For technical issues, please email portal@jnhs.edu.ph or visit the school office during business hours. You can also reach us through our Facebook page.
