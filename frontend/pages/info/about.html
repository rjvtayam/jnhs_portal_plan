Our Mission

To develop globally competitive students equipped with 21st century skills, good moral values, and a strong sense of community service through quality DepEd-standardized education.

Our Vision

A leading public secondary school in Quezon Province producing academically excellent, morally upright, and socially responsible learners committed to national development.

History

Jomalig National High School was established to serve the educational needs of the youth in the island municipality of Jomalig, Quezon. Located in the heart of the community, the school has been a pillar of education, providing free secondary education to students from Grades 7 to 12 under the DepEd K-12 program.

Over the years, JNHS has grown to become one of the recognized public secondary schools in the Division of Quezon, consistently producing graduates who excel in academics, sports, and community service.
