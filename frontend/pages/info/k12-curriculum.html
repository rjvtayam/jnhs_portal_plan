K-12 Curriculum

The Philippine Basic Education Curriculum under the Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013 (RA 10533)

What is K-12?

The K to 12 Program covers Kindergarten, 6 years of elementary education, 4 years of junior high school (Grades 7 to 10), and 2 years of senior high school (Grades 11 to 12). It was implemented to give Filipino students sufficient time to master core academic competencies, develop lifelong learning skills, and prepare for higher education, employment, or entrepreneurship.

Senior High School Tracks

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics - For students pursuing careers in engineering, medicine, IT, and sciences.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management - For students pursuing careers in business, accounting, and management.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences - For students pursuing careers in law, education, social work, and media.

GAS

General Academic Strand - For students who are still exploring their career path or need a flexible academic track.

Core Subjects (JHS)

Junior High School Core Curriculum

The Junior High School curriculum includes the following core subjects:

Filipino, English, Mathematics, Science, Araling Panlipunan, MAPEH (Music, Arts, Physical Education, Health), Edukasyon sa Pagkatao, Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE)

Senior High School Core Subjects

The Senior High School curriculum includes core subjects common across all tracks:

Oral Communication, Reading and Writing, 21st Century Literature, Komunikasyon at Pananaliksik, Contemporary Philippine Arts, Disaster Readiness, Physical Education, Research, Practical Research, Personal Development, Understanding Culture and Society
