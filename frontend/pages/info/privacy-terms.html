Privacy Policy & Terms

Important information about how the JNHS Portal collects, uses, and protects your data.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Information We Collect

The JNHS Portal collects the following information for educational purposes:

  • Student Information: Name, LRN (Learner Reference Number), grade level, section, enrollment status, and academic records (grades, attendance).
  • Teacher Information: Name, employee ID, department, and assigned subjects/sections.
  • Parent Information: Name, email address, and relationship to student.
  • User Account Data: Username, password (encrypted), role, and login activity.

How We Use Your Information

  • Manage student enrollment, grades, and attendance records.
  • Generate DepEd-compliant reports (SF9 Report Card, SF10 Permanent Record).
  • Communicate school announcements, events, and academic updates.
  • Provide parents access to their child's academic progress.
  • Maintain system security and monitor portal usage.

Data Protection

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm.
  • Access to student records is restricted based on user roles.
  • System access requires authenticated login with JWT tokens.
  • Data is stored securely in the school's PostgreSQL database.
  • Only authorized school personnel can access student information.

Data Sharing

Your personal information is not shared, sold, or distributed to third parties. Data is only accessible to authorized school personnel (teachers, registrar, admin, principal) for legitimate educational purposes.

Terms and Conditions

By accessing and using the JNHS Portal, you agree to the following terms:

Acceptable Use

  • The portal is intended for educational use by enrolled students, parents, teachers, and authorized school staff.
  • Users must not attempt to access accounts other than their own.
  • Users must not share their login credentials with others.
  • Users must not attempt to harm, disrupt, or compromise the portal's security.

Account Responsibilities

  • Students: Your username is your LRN. Keep your account secure and do not share your password.
  • Parents: Your username is your email address. Your password is your child's LRN. Contact the registrar for account issues.
  • Teachers & Staff: Your account is issued by the registrar/admin. Report any security concerns immediately.

Account Suspension

The school reserves the right to suspend or deactivate accounts that:

  • Are used for unauthorized purposes.
  • Attempt to access restricted areas or data.
  • Violate the school's acceptable use policy.
  • Compromise the security of the portal or other users' data.

Limitation of Liability

The JNHS Portal is provided "as is" for educational purposes. While we strive to maintain accuracy, the school is not liable for any errors in grades or data displayed. For official records, please refer to the school registrar's office.
